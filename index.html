1. Introduction

Welcome to Melas Hotels. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you interact with our services, including our AI-powered customer service system, WhatsApp communications, and automated booking processes through n8n workflow automation.

By using our services, visiting our properties, or communicating with us through any channel (including WhatsApp, our website, or AI chat systems), you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, contact details (phone number, email address, postal address)
  • Passport/ID information for hotel registration
  • Payment and billing information
  • Booking preferences and special requests
  • Communication history and preferences

2.2 WhatsApp & Communication Data

  • WhatsApp phone numbers and profile information
  • Message content and conversation history
  • Media files shared (photos, documents, voice messages)
  • Message timestamps and delivery status
  • Device information and IP addresses

2.3 AI Interaction Data

  • Chat conversations with our AI assistant
  • Questions asked and responses provided
  • User preferences and behavior patterns
  • Session data and interaction timestamps

2.4 Automated Processing Data (n8n)

  • Booking workflow data and status
  • Automated email and message triggers
  • Customer journey and touchpoint data
  • Integration data between different systems

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Processing hotel bookings and reservations
  • Providing customer support through AI and human agents
  • Managing your stay and hotel services
  • Facilitating communication via WhatsApp and other channels

3.2 AI-Powered Services

  • Training and improving our AI customer service system
  • Providing personalized recommendations and responses
  • Automating routine inquiries and bookings
  • Analyzing conversation patterns to enhance service quality

3.3 Automated Workflows (n8n)

  • Streamlining booking confirmations and updates
  • Sending automated notifications and reminders
  • Coordinating between different hotel systems
  • Managing customer communication workflows

Data Processing for AI Training

Your conversations with our AI system may be used to improve the quality and accuracy of our automated responses. All data used for training purposes is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information.

We use WhatsApp Business API through Meta's Cloud infrastructure. Your WhatsApp communications are processed according to:

  • Meta's Data Policy and Terms of Service
  • End-to-end encryption for message content
  • Metadata processing for service delivery
  • Compliance with Meta's business messaging policies

4.2 n8n Workflow Automation

We use n8n for automating various business processes. This includes:

  • Secure data processing within our infrastructure
  • Integration with hotel management systems
  • Automated email and messaging services
  • Customer data synchronization across platforms

4.3 AI Service Providers

Our AI customer service may involve third-party AI providers who:

  • Process conversations to generate responses
  • Maintain data security and privacy standards
  • Comply with applicable data protection regulations
  • Do not use your data for their own commercial purposes

5. Data Security & Protection

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Secure API connections and data transmission
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures

5.2 WhatsApp Security

  • End-to-end encryption for message content
  • Secure API connections with Meta's servers

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Data Subject Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Choose preferred communication channels
  • Control AI interaction settings
  • Manage WhatsApp message preferences

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for different periods depending on the type of data:

  • Booking Data: 7 years for accounting and legal purposes
  • WhatsApp Messages: 1 year unless longer retention is required
  • AI Conversation Data: 2 years for service improvement
  • Marketing Data: Until you withdraw consent
  • Legal Requirements: As required by applicable law

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including:
